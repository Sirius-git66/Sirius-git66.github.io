EIGHT SPECIALISED QUANTITATIVE AGENTS • ONE DIRECTIONAL FLOW

A transparent relay engineered for trust

Think of it as eight specialists working in a relay. Each one tallies, measures, calibrates or nets your book, then passes the baton cleanly to the next — from raw positions to a clear funding plan.

01

Position Aggregator

Gathers every position you hold — cargoes, futures, swaps, inventory — into one clean picture of what you're exposed to.

02

Risk Engine (FHS)

Works out how much your positions could realistically lose in a day.

03

Stress Testing

Replays historical crisis scenarios and hypothetical shocks to size the 'bad day' buffer.

04

Exchange Margin Calculator

Translates the risk into an indicative estimate of each exchange's requirement, from its own published rates.

05

Liquidity & Concentration

Adds estimated add-ons for positions that are big or hard to exit quickly.

06

Portfolio Cross-Margin

Models correlation offsets across your book so the internal estimate isn't overstated, using estimated co-movements between markets.

07

Bubble & Scenario Simulator

Monitors for statistically explosive prices, plus 'what if' scenario testing on your own book.

08

Treasury & Reporting

Turns everything into a plan: what you're likely to owe, and when, and a cost-ranked way to fund it.
